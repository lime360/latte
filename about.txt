Latte is a text-editor that replicates Notepad

Originally v1.5 was planned to be the last version of Latte, but then i changed my
mind and finished it completely to be the remake of Notepad

Developed by lime360 using Clickteam Fusion 2.5

P.S. You can also check out Espresso, which is a modification of Latte that features
Lua scripting, live HTML editor and lots of more features!
